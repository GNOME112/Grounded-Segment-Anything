# Code of Conduct

## Community Standards
- We are committed to providing a welcoming and friendly environment for all community members.
- All interactions should be respectful and courteous. Discrimination and harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
- Be considerate in your communication. Ponder how your words may affect others.

## Professional Conduct Guidelines
- Engage in healthy debates, be open to feedback, and be willing to learn from others.
- Maintain professionalism when discussing issues or disagreements. Keep discussions constructive.
- No trolling, personal attacks, or inflammatory comments.

## Conflict Resolution Procedures
1. **Calm the Situation**: Reach out to the involved parties privately and attempt to resolve the issue calmly and respectfully.
2. **Involve a Moderator**: If the issue cannot be resolved between the involved parties, please request the assistance of a moderator or community leader.
3. **Escalate if Necessary**: If further escalation is required, a formal report can be submitted to the governing body of the community.
4. **Follow Up**: After resolving the conflict, check in with all involved parties to ensure the situation has been fully addressed and no lingering issues remain.
